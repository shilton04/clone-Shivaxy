Shivaxy pharmaceuticals private limited came into existence with a motive to provide affordable health care facility across globe and was founded with a vision to emerge as a leading integrated research-based, global pharmaceutical company. Our branded generics business has a significant presence in markets across emerging economies.

At Shivaxy Pharmaceuticals, we ensure that the products are manufactured with high quality standards which are maintained at each stage of the manufacturing process at our facilities. We ensure that our manufacturing facilities are committed to implement good manufacturing practices and good laboratory practices as per the medical norms set by the medical fraternity and WHO-GMP.
We engage in the research, development, manufacturing, sourcing, marketing, and distribution of high-quality pharmaceutical products. We are a fully integrated company with in-house business development, R&D, manufacturing and regulatory compliance capabilities.
